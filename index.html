Location
Which neighbourhood would you want the property in?
Click a card to see it on the map. Each area offers a different balance of rental yield, lifestyle, and what $400K actually buys you.
Copacabana
Example apartment
CopacabanaR$12,000–14,000/m²
Rio's most iconic 4km beach strip. Best short-term rental liquidity in the city, with huge international demand year-round. More affordable per m² than Ipanema, meaning more space for the same money.
What $400K gets you: A spacious 4-bedroom apartment, ~120–160m². Often partly sea-facing, modernised but not necessarily brand new.
Leme
Example apartment
LemeSimilar to Copa
The quieter, more residential end of Copacabana beach, feels local rather than touristy. Has produced the best-performing Airbnb listings in our research: $44.6K/yr at 60% occupancy for a 3BR.
What $400K gets you: A 3–4 bedroom apartment. Potentially the strongest Airbnb returns of any neighbourhood at this price point.
Ipanema
Example apartment
IpanemaR$20,000–25,000/m²
The most upscale beach neighbourhood. Attracts higher-paying guests, premium nightly rates, and a more curated lifestyle with the best restaurants, cafes and bars in the city. Budget stretches less far.
What $400K gets you: A 3-bedroom apartment, ~80–100m². Less space but a premium address that commands higher nightly rates.
Santa Teresa
Example apartment
Santa TeresaR$11,000/m²
A bohemian hilltop neighbourhood with colonial architecture, artists' studios and the best bar scene in the city. No beach access, but sweeping views over Rio. The money goes much further here than anywhere in Zona Sul.
What $400K gets you: A 5–6 bedroom house, ~350–450m², often with garden, terraces and views. The most space for the money by far.
Botafogo
Example apartment
Botafogo / FlamengoR$13,000–16,000/m²
A fast-gentrifying neighbourhood between Ipanema and the city centre. Strong tech/startup scene, excellent metro links. Not a swimming beach but great for longer stays, better value and increasingly popular with international visitors.
What $400K gets you: A large 4-bedroom apartment, ~130–160m², often with higher-end finishes than Copa at a similar price.
